WELCOME MESSAGE FROM MY SCHOOL

Dear parents, guardians and everyone!

On behalf of the teaching and non-teaching staff of MY SCHOOL, we welcome you to our website.

I'm pleased to be leading MY SCHOOL through such an exhilarating time in education.

At MY SCHOOL, we aim to give every child the best foundation possible to their education through exciting and engaging interactions inside and outside the classrooms, enabling them to become active learners through a holistic and broad-based curriculum.

The Preschool years are crucial to a child's development and our Nursery, Kinder 1 and Kinder 2 stages enable the children to get their school careers off to a soaring start. Moreover, our Primary classes enjoy varied and wide experiences which bring the different subjects to life and make learning enjoyable. Every member of the MY SCHOOL family gives their best to guarantee excellent quality of education for the children in every level.
